functional requirements:

1. Biometric Enrollment:

The system shall allow students to securely enroll their biometric data (e.g., fingerprints, facial recognition).


2. Attendance Recording:

Students shall be able to mark their attendance using their enrolled biometric data.

3. Attendance Management:

Faculty members shall be able to view and manage attendance records for their classes.

4. Real-time Updates:

The system shall provide real-time attendance updates to faculty and administrators.

5. Reports and Analytics:

The system shall generate attendance reports and analytics for educational institutions.

6. Data Privacy:

The system shall ensure that student biometric data is securely stored and only accessible to authorized personnel.

7. Integration with Existing Systems:

If applicable, the system shall integrate with existing student information systems to retrieve student data.

8. Scalability:

The system shall be able to handle a growing number of students and attendance records as the educational institution expands.

9. Access Control:

The system shall implement role-based access control, allowing different user types (e.g., students, faculty, administrators) to access relevant features.

10.Notification System:

The system shall notify students and faculty of important attendance-related events, such as low attendance or missed classes.
