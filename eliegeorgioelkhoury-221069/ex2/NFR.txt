non-functional requirements:

1. Security:

Biometric data shall be securely stored and transmitted using encryption and access controls.

2. Scalability:

The system shall be capable of scaling horizontally and vertically to handle increased user loads and data volumes.

3. Performance:

The system shall provide fast response times for recording attendance and generating reports.

4. Reliability:

The system shall have high availability and minimal downtime, ensuring continuous access.

5. Compliance:

The system shall comply with data privacy regulations (e.g., GDPR, FERPA) and adhere to educational institution policies.
